Fast & efficient

Make sure your drinks and keep up with orders, and the faster and provide more efficient service, More customers able to serve. Will want to return to a place which provides quality coffee in a quick manner.

Customer focused

Could read and write tons of articles on customer service, and I’m pretty sure you can conclude the importance of being customer focused and putting their needs first. A good barista does just that—puts customer needs first and wants to make people feel special and surpass their needs.

Clean & Attentive

Cleanliness and sanitation is very important in the food industry. As Colette says in Ratatouille, you must keep your station clean!! Having high standards of hygiene and keeping areas clean is a great trait to have. Organization wins over sloppy carelessness any day.

Waiter and waitress Service

Welcome guests with a smile and friendly disposition. Promptly guide guests to their table and ensure everything is comfortable. Provide water, menus, and any complimentary items such as bread rolls or chips upon seating. Allow guests time to look over the menu and be available to answer questions.

Banquet Service

Setting and staging a nice event is only half the battle; serving guests with appropriate standards and etiquette is equally as important. Standards incorporate the ways in which staff and servers present themselves and food to guests. Here are some basic guidelines for ensuring the satisfaction of event guests.

Buffet Service

Station-type buffet – Waiters serve the beverages while guests get the other dishes from the buffet line. Modified deluxe buffet – Tables are set with cutlery; waiters serve the beverages, coffee, and dessert while guests get the other dishes from the buffet line.

Semi-Self Service

A self-service restaurant is any dining establishment where diners serve themselves food or drinks and pay for the food before dining. There are many styles of self-service restaurants. With fast food dining, diners order from a cashier or a self-service kiosk.
